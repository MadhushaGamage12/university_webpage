About Us

We are the world largest university

Established in 2000 as part of a joint venture between Metatechno Inc., London,New York ,Japan, and kingdom Group of Companies, Horizon university continues to set a benchmark in the field of IT studies. Located in the heart of Colombo, Horizon university is a Higher Education institute with degree-awarding status in South Asia to offer the unique package and We offer government-approved B.Sc (Hons) in Software Engineering and Bachelor of Science (Hons) Computing (Information Systems) degrees. The demand for IT engineers in the world is increasing very rapidly. Therefore, as a IT university, HORIZON is devoted to training our energetic youth with purposefully built degree programmes to cater to this growing demand in the world. Properly trained HORIZON graduates will be able to join the lucrative IT industry after the successful completion of study programs and job-related examinations at HORIZON. Those who are interested in pursuing further studies at the postgraduate level will also be able to join our partner universities in London,New York and Japan.
