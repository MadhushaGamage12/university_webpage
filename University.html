Our facilities

Welcome to Horizon University, where excellence meets opportunity. Our facilities are designed to provide a comprehensive and enriching experience for all our students.

Library

Our state of the library offers an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources, ensuring that you have access to all the materials needed for your academic pursuits. With comfortable study spaces and expert staff, our library is the ideal environment for learning and research.

Sports Complex

Stay fit and active with our modern sports complex, featuring a well-equipped gym, swimming pool, and courts for various sports including basketball, tennis, and volleyball. Our facilities support both recreational activities and competitive sports, encouraging a healthy and balanced lifestyle.

Cafeteria

Enjoy a variety of delicious and nutritious meals at our cafeteria, which caters to diverse dietary needs and preferences. From traditional dishes to international cuisine, our dining services ensure that you have access to healthy and satisfying food throughout the day.
